Seoul Data Atlas

An interactive, map-first atlas exploring how extreme heat reshapes retail activity across Seoul — combining the Urban Heat / Urban Sales (UHUS) datasets into a three-dimensional geospatial dashboard of all 25 districts and 422 administrative dongs.
